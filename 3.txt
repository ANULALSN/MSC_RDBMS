4. Project Information with Manager Details
Projects in "COIMBATORE":
sql
SELECT
    P.PNUMBER AS Project_Number,
    P.DNUMBER AS Department_Number,
    E.NAME AS Manager_Name,
    E.HOUSENAME AS Manager_House,
    E.BIRTHDATE AS Manager_Birthdate
FROM
    PROJECT P
JOIN
    DEPARTMENT D ON P.DNUMBER = D.DNUMBER
JOIN
    EMPLOYEE E ON D.MANAGERID = E.EMPLOYEEID
WHERE
    P.PLOCATION = 'COIMBATORE';
text
+----------------+-------------------+--------------+---------------+-------------------+
| Project_Number | Department_Number | Manager_Name | Manager_House | Manager_Birthdate |
+----------------+-------------------+--------------+---------------+-------------------+
|              3 |               101 | SHOBHANA     | SEVAMANDHIR   | 1996-12-12        |
+----------------+-------------------+--------------+---------------+-------------------+

5. Employees Working on Multiple Projects
sql
SELECT 
    E.EMPLOYEEID,
    E.NAME,
    COUNT(W.PNUMBER) AS NUMBER_OF_PROJECTS 
FROM 
    EMPLOYEE E 
JOIN 
    WORKS_ON W ON E.EMPLOYEEID = W.EMPLOYEEID 
GROUP BY 
    E.EMPLOYEEID, E.NAME 
HAVING 
    COUNT(W.PNUMBER) > 1;
text
+------------+---------+--------------------+
| EMPLOYEEID | NAME    | NUMBER_OF_PROJECTS |
+------------+---------+--------------------+
|        305 | PRITHVI |                  2 |
+------------+---------+--------------------+
